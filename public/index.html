📚 How to Use InstantCal

Transform any email into a calendar event in seconds! Follow these simple steps:

1
📧

Paste Your Email

Copy and paste your meeting invitation, event email, or any text containing event details into the input area. You can use either:

  • Rich Content: Paste directly from your email client (preserves formatting)
  • Plain Text: Copy just the text content
2
🤖

AI Processing

Click "Generate Calendar Event" and our AI will instantly analyze your text to extract:

  • 📅 Event title and description
  • 🕐 Start and end times
  • 📍 Location details
  • 👥 Attendee information
  • 🔗 Meeting links
3
📅

Download & Import

Review the extracted details and download your calendar file:

  • Desktop: Click "Download .ics" and import to your calendar app
  • iPhone: Tap "Add to iPhone Calendar" for direct integration
  • Edit: Click "Edit Details" to modify any information before downloading
